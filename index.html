Which statement best describes how the properties of water contribute to capillary action in plants?

A) High specific heat prevents rapid temperature changes.
B) Cohesion and adhesion allow water to defy gravity.
C) Water acts as a universal solvent for nonpolar molecules.
D) Ice is less dense than liquid water.
Explanation: Cohesion allows water molecules to stick to each other, while adhesion allows them to stick to the xylem walls, pulling the water column upward.
